# DonutChart

An allocation / part-to-whole chart. It renders a ring of segments sized by
their share of the total, drawn as an SVG.

> View in [Figma](https://www.figma.com/design/JxaLVMTWirCpU0rsbZ30k7/2.-Components-Library?node-id=17763-34880).

## Usage

`DonutChart` owns its `series` data and renders exactly the segments it
receives — grouping ("top N + other") is handled outside the component. Each
segment's angle is its `value` as a fraction of the summed total; segments are
laid out in series order, clockwise from 12 o'clock, with rounded caps and gaps.

## Basics

Define your data as an array of `DonutSegment`s. Each segment needs a stable
`id`, a `label`, and a raw `value`; `color` is optional.
